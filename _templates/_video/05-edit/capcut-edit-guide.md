# Edit Guide — Step 8 (human, CapCut)

**Golden rules:** cut on a breath/sentence break (never mid-phrase); alternate Ken Burns motion
(never the same move twice in a row); a pattern interrupt every 20–30s; the mid-video re-hook beat.

## Settings
- **Ken Burns:** keyframe scale/position; zoom 5–15% or pan 15–20%; ease in/out. Subtle, not distracting.
- **Captions:** auto-caption, then proofread (proper nouns/dates). On-brand font; readable on mobile.
- **Music:** one Content-ID-safe bed, low under narration (~ -18 to -24 dB); lift in silent beats.
- **Pattern interrupts:** new graphic / text pop / music shift every 20–30s.
- **End screen:** last 5–20s; 2–4 elements (subscribe + ONE specific "watch next" + playlist).

## Export
- 1080p (1920×1080), H.264, 30fps, ~12–16 Mbps. **No watermark** (CapCut free exports clean).
- Optional: unlisted test upload to confirm **no Content-ID claim** on the music before going public.
